About me

My name is Tobias Sundstrand and I'am a mobile developer consultant working out of Stockholm. I work with both iOS and Android and have been doing so since 2009. Meetups are a great way to meet other developers and refine your skills, I frequently attend Cocoa Heads Stockholm and occasionally go to other meetups as well. The technology market, even just the mobile market, is evolving very fast. Keeping up with a never ending stream of news is one of my passions and also really important to be able to deliver stable, maintainable and awesome apps on time. The Open Source communities for iOS and Android have really helped me while developing all applications I have worked on and I would really like to start contributing more to those communities.
